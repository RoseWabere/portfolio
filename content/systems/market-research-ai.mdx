## Overview

An AI assistant that analyzes market reports and identifies emerging niches. It uses LangChain to chain document parsing, entity extraction, and niche identification, then generates actionable insights.

## System Architecture

- **Document Ingestion:** PDF and DOCX parsing
- **Chunking:** 1,000-token chunks with overlap
- **LLM:** OpenAI Function Calling to extract structured data (e.g., market size, growth rate, competitors)
- **Output:** JSON report with ranked opportunities

## Results

- Processed 1,000+ reports in 2 hours (vs 80 hours manual)
- Identified 12 new niches for clients in gaming and retail
- Helped launch two new product lines
