## Overview

A data pipeline that scrapes food prices from Kenyan e-commerce sites and government reports, processes them with PySpark, and visualizes inflation trends in Grafana.

## System Architecture

- **Scraping:** Python with BeautifulSoup/Scrapy
- **Processing:** PySpark for aggregation and anomaly detection
- **Storage:** PostgreSQL partitioned by month
- **Forecasting:** Prophet models for price predictions
- **Dashboard:** Grafana with alerts for abnormal price changes

## Impact

- Used by a local economics blog for weekly reports
- Helped small businesses negotiate bulk purchase timing
- Open data source for researchers
